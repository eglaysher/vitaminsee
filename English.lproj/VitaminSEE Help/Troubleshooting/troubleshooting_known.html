Known Problems in VitaminSEE

  • After changing into a new directory, there is a small window (usually less then 2 seconds) when thumbnails for files other then the first two visible in the list are not renerd. They are quickly displayed afterwards. This problem occurs because of a hack I put in that forces extra redraws on the image list. This hack prevents image corruption--before I added this hack, thumbnail's would often draw ontop of other thumbnails because of what I'm guessing is a bug in NSScrollView.
  • After renaming a file, thumbnails for other files aren't displayed until you click on the main window.
